This program simulates traffic on a road.  The rectangles are stop lights and the squares are cars. 
You must have atleast 1 car and stoplight and can have up to 3 cars and 3 stoplights.

User Guide:
1. Choose the number of cars and stoplights you would like then click start.
2. The simulation will start.  To add more cars or lights, click the "Add Car" or "Add light" button on the right.
3. To stop/restart the program, click the "Stop/Restart" button at the botton.  
   To pause/resume the program, click the "Pause/Resume" button at the botton.
